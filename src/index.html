Drone

Drone is harmonic or monophonic

Drone is a project created by Glenn E. Bailey III that aims to collaborate and create unique music with various forms of bagpipes and other instruments. The music is inspired by folk traditions of the current and past, including, but not limited to, Renaissance, Scottish, Irish, Breton, and Medieval.
